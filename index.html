Our Mission and Vision

"To generate new knowledge via engaging in cutting-edge research and to promote academic growth by offering state-of-the-art under graduate, post graduate programs.To identify, based on an informed perception of Nepalese, regional and global needs, areas specialization upon which the college can concentrate.To undertake collaborative projects which offer opportunities for long term interaction with academic excellence. We aim to rise to the top and provide quality education as well as exceptional skills to every to-be-engineer with our advanced facilities and a myriad of other educational materials. To contribute to the nation and the world through excellence in scientific and technical education and research; to serve as a valuable resource for industry and society; and remain a source of pride for all Nepalese.To develop human potential to its fullest extent so that intellectually capable and imaginatively gifted can emerge in a range of professions."
